Altria Pendragon

A legendary king of Britain. Also called King of Knights. Altria is a childhood name and, upon being raised as a king, she began to be called King Arthur. In a age when chivalry had lost its beauty, with a holy sword in her hands, she brought about a brief moment of peace and final prosperity to Britain. A man in historical fact, but it seems that in this world she was a cross-dressing beauty.
